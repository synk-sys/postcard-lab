Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Postcard Lab, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following:

  • Photos — the image you upload for your postcard.
  • Messages — the text you write to appear on the back of your postcard.
  • Recipient address — the name and mailing address of the person you're sending to.
  • Your name and email address — so we can send you a confirmation and tracking information.
  • Payment information — processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and print your postcard order.
  • To send you a confirmation email and tracking ID.
  • To process your payment via Stripe.
  • To communicate with you about your order if an issue arises.

Third Parties We Share Data With

PostGrid — Our printing and mailing partner. Your photo, message, and recipient address are transmitted to PostGrid to fulfill your order. PostGrid is a Canadian company subject to PIPEDA.

Stripe — Our payment processor. Your payment information is handled exclusively by Stripe and subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party, and we do not use it for advertising.

Data Retention

Order data (including photos and messages) is retained for up to 30 days after fulfillment, then permanently deleted from our systems. Payment records are retained as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at hello@postcardlab.ca to exercise these rights.

Delivery

Delivery

Estimated Delivery Times

Typically 5–10 business days via Canada Post, depending on your location within Canada.

Delivery Area

We currently deliver to addresses within Canada only, including all 13 provinces and territories. International delivery is not available at this time.

Carrier Delays

Postcard Lab is not responsible for delays caused by Canada Post or other factors outside our control, including (but not limited to) extreme weather, postal strikes, statutory holidays, or incorrect address information provided by the customer.

Lost Mail

In the event your postcard does not arrive within 14 business days, please contact us and we will investigate with Canada Post on your behalf. We are not liable for mail lost by the carrier after it has been handed over for delivery.

Address Errors

You are responsible for ensuring the recipient's address is accurate. We cannot reroute or replace postcards sent to an incorrect address due to customer error, though we will do our best to assist where possible.

Refunds

Refunds & Cancellations

All Sales Final

Once your order has been sent to print (typically within 1 business day of placing your order), it cannot be cancelled or refunded, as production begins immediately.

When We Will Issue a Refund

We will issue a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • Delivery failure — if your postcard has not been delivered within 14 business days of the mailing date and we are unable to locate it via Canada Post.
  • Printing error — if we made a verifiable error in printing your postcard (e.g., wrong photo, wrong message, or significant print quality defect that is clearly our fault).

To request a refund, email hello@postcardlab.ca with your order tracking ID and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

We are not responsible for carrier delays, lost mail after handover to Canada Post, or address errors made by the customer.
